What Is Professional Air Duct Cleaning?

Professional air duct cleaning is a comprehensive service that removes dust, debris, mold spores, allergens, and pollutants from your HVAC system’s ductwork. At WellDuct Cleaning, our technicians use high-powered HEPA-filter vacuum systems, brushes, and source removal tools to meticulously clean supply and return ducts, registers, coils, and air handling units. It’s more effective—and safer—than a DIY approach.

Why Choose a Professional Over DIY?

Attempting to clean air ducts yourself can be messy and incomplete, often damaging duct materials or voiding warranties. Signs You Need Professional Air Duct Cleaning

It’s easy to overlook your ductwork until problems start showing. But waiting too long can lead to health issues and expensive HVAC repairs. Here are clear signs it’s time to call WellDuct Cleaning:

  • Dust Buildup: If your furniture gets dusty quickly even after cleaning, your ducts may be circulating debris.
  • Allergy Flare-Ups: Increased sneezing, coughing, or asthma attacks can be caused by mold and allergens inside your duct system.
  • Strange Odors: Musty smells when your HVAC runs may indicate mold or mildew inside your ductwork.
  • Poor Airflow: Uneven room temperatures or weak airflow may mean your ducts are clogged or damaged.
  • Recent Renovations: Construction dust and drywall particles often settle in ductwork and should be removed professionally.
